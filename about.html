ABOUT US

About us

At United Helping Hand Bahuuddeshiya Sanstha, we are dedicated to uplifting communities through various initiatives focused on employment generation, education, health, and social welfare. Through our concerted efforts, we strive to create a positive impact and foster sustainable development.

Employment Generation

Skills Development Programs

We conduct skill development workshops and training programs aimed at enhancing employability and fostering entrepreneurship among individuals from marginalized communities.

Job Placement Services

We conduct skill development workshops and training programs aimed at enhancing employability and fostering entrepreneurship among individuals from marginalized communities.

Education

Educational Support

We provide educational support to children from underprivileged backgrounds, including access to school supplies, tutoring services, and scholarships to ensure they have equal opportunities for learning.

Adult Literacy Programs

Recognizing the importance of lifelong learning, we offer adult literacy programs to empower individuals with essential literacy and numeracy skills, enabling them to participate more actively in society.

Health

Health Awareness Campaigns

We organize health awareness campaigns and workshops to promote preventive healthcare practices and raise awareness about prevalent health issues within the community.

Medical Camps

Our organization conducts regular medical camps, providing free medical check-ups, consultations, and essential healthcare services to individuals who lack access to adequate healthcare facilities.

Social Welfare

Community Development Projects

We initiate community development projects focusing on infrastructure development, sanitation, and environmental conservation to enhance the overall well-being of the community.

Crisis Intervention

United Helping Hand Bahuuddeshiya Sanstha extends support to individuals and families during times of crisis, offering relief assistance and social support to help them overcome challenging situations.

Join us in our mission to create a more inclusive and equitable society. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of those in need.
